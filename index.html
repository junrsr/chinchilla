I'm a prey.

I'd prefer not to fight, and instead I naturally dart for cover, freeze or cower to potential threats. However, if in the face of danger, I can quickly lose fur, giving me time to escape.






We're just like humans!

Although we cower in the face of danger, we are highly sociable animals! We can communicate through a variety of high-pitched grunting noises and we have hearing ranges similar to humans.






I'm nocturnal.

I typically sleep through the day, and I'm most active during the evening and night. I normally sleep upright in a huddled, tight position, but can also sleep sideways and even upside down!
